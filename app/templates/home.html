Publish to GitHub Pages

Free hosting for your portfolio - no credit card needed

Create a GitHub Account

Sign up at github.com if you don't have an account. It's free and takes 2 minutes!

Create a New Repository

Click the + icon → New repository. Name it username.github.io (replace 'username' with your GitHub username).

Upload Your Portfolio

Rename your downloaded portfolio file to index.html. Click "Add file" → "Upload files" and drop your index.html file.

Enable GitHub Pages

Go to Settings → Pages → Source: select "main" branch → Save. Your site will be live at username.github.io in 2-3 minutes!

Share Your Link

Add your portfolio URL to your resume, LinkedIn, and job applications. Update anytime by uploading a new index.html file!
